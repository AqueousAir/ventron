We may collect and process the following types of personal data:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name
  • Email address

  • Country or location

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain technical data may be collected automatically, including:

This information helps us improve website performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries and quote requests
  • Providing information about our products and services
  • Improving website functionality and user experience
  • Managing business communications
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Preventing fraud or security threats

We only process personal data when we have a lawful basis under GDPR.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases to process your personal data:

  • Consent — when you voluntarily provide information through forms
  • Legitimate Interest — for business communications and website improvements
  • Contractual Necessity — when processing is required to fulfill a service request
  • Legal Obligations — when required by law

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

VENTRON ENERGY does not sell or rent personal data.

However, we may share data with trusted third parties when necessary, including:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Cloud service providers
  • CRM or communication platforms
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, etc.)

All third parties are required to comply with applicable data protection regulations.

6. International Data Transfers

Because VENTRON ENERGY operates internationally, personal data may be processed or stored outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with GDPR.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
  • comply with legal obligations
  • resolve disputes and enforce agreements

After this period, personal data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@ventronenergy.com.

You also have the right to file a complaint with a data protection authority in the EU.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and analyze traffic.

Cookies may be used for:

  • website analytics
  • performance monitoring
  • remembering user preferences

You can control cookie settings through your browser.

10. Data Security

VENTRON ENERGY implements appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data from:

  • unauthorized access

  • alteration or misuse

Despite these measures, no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites and encourage users to review their privacy policies.
