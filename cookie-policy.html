2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website.

Cookies help websites function properly, improve performance, and provide information to website owners about how users interact with the site.

Cookies may be stored temporarily (session cookies) or for a longer period (persistent cookies).

3. Types of Cookies We Use

VENTRON ENERGY may use the following categories of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled in our systems.

They may include cookies used to:

  • maintain website security
  • enable page navigation
  • manage form submissions
  • provide basic functionality

Without these cookies, some parts of the website may not work properly.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information such as:

  • number of visitors
  • pages visited
  • time spent on pages
  • traffic sources

This information helps us improve website functionality and user experience.

Examples may include tools such as:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics platforms.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow the website to remember choices you make, such as:

  • language preferences
  • region settings
  • previously submitted forms

These cookies improve the usability of the website.

Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies may be used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements or marketing content.

VENTRON ENERGY currently does not actively use advertising tracking cookies, but they may be implemented in the future for marketing campaigns.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services that appear on our website, such as:

These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

VENTRON ENERGY does not control the use of these cookies.

5. How Long Cookies Are Stored

Cookies may remain on your device for different durations:

Session Cookies
Deleted automatically when you close your browser.

Persistent Cookies
Remain stored on your device until they expire or are manually deleted.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • block cookies
  • delete stored cookies
  • receive notifications when cookies are set

Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Browser instructions can typically be found here:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
